# AIRCA Intellectual Lineage

AIRCA should be explicit about what it synthesizes.

## Major strands

### Prior decision model work
AIRCA should absorb and operationalize prior decision-model thinking into reusable artifacts, examples, and workflow rules.

### Decision intelligence
AIRCA aligns with the idea that organizations need better decisions, not just better analytics.

### Bounded rationality and bias
AIRCA should assume that attention, time, and cognition are limited.

### Game theory and mechanism design
AIRCA should help structure multi-actor decisions with better incentives, review logic, and escalation rules.

## AIRCA-specific contribution

The repository-specific contribution should be framed as:

- self-learning human-in-the-loop decision architecture
- machine-readable decision artifacts
- white-paper generation from real decisions
- cognitive load remediation through structured decision flows
- scalable human agency in AI-assisted environments
