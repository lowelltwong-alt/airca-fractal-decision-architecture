# White Paper Generation

AIRCA does not need a traditional white paper before it becomes useful.

Instead, AIRCA should generate white-paper-style documents from real decisions.

## Why this matters

This approach creates:

- real examples instead of abstract claims
- reusable knowledge from actual work
- publishable artifacts for blogs, internal memos, and papers
- a feedback loop between decisions and thought leadership

## AIRCA white paper flow

1. Make a structured AIRCA decision
2. Review the artifact with a human if needed
3. Add learning signals and review horizon
4. Publish a white-paper-style markdown output
5. Revisit the decision and refine future guidance

## Result

AIRCA becomes both:

- a decision operating system
- a white-paper-generating system
