# Human Agency and Cognitive Load

AIRCA is not meant to replace human judgment.

It is meant to make human judgment more scalable, less overloaded, and more durable.

## Why this matters

Many organizations do not fail because they lack information.

They fail because they lack:

- decision clarity
- clear ownership
- explicit tradeoffs
- consistent review
- usable memory of past choices

## AIRCA position

AIRCA should be positioned as a system that:

- reduces decision fatigue
- reduces re-deciding the same issue
- reduces context loss
- reduces cognitive switching costs inside knowledge work
- increases the leverage of human reviewers and decision owners

## Human-in-the-loop principle

AIRCA should preserve human control over:

- framing
- escalation
- final commitment
- review of high-stakes outputs
- retrospective learning

## Desirability principle

To be desirable in the market, AIRCA must make users feel:

- faster
- clearer
- more confident
- more accountable
- less overwhelmed
