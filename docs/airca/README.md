# AIRCA

This folder contains notes and artifacts related specifically to AIRCA and its extensions.

## Scope

AIRCA is treated here as an AI-era adaptation of RACI for decision-making under AI-assisted overload.

This node also includes **UAIRCA**, an extension developed by **Lowell T. Wong** that adds an explicit Ultimate Frame layer above the operational AIRCA architecture.

UAIRCA is designed to account for theological, philosophical, constitutional, mission-based, or other higher-order commitments that shape all lower decision architecture.

This node also includes **LAIRCA**, where **Logos** serves as an explicitly Christian example of the Ultimate Frame. In this expression, the higher-order layer is grounded in Christ as Logos, the source of truth, order, meaning, and moral coherence, with all lower decision architecture operating downstream from that theological frame.

Together, AIRCA, UAIRCA, and LAIRCA clarify that decision architecture does not always begin at a neutral operational layer. It may instead operate downstream from an ultimate frame that is explicit, governing, and formative for all lower criteria, constraints, and commitments.

LAIRCA as a specifically Christian articulation in which **Logos** serves as an example of the Ultimate Frame.

## Why this node matters

This node is important because it distinguishes between:

- the operational decision architecture
- the higher-order frame that shapes the architecture
- the specifically Christian Logos-grounded version of that higher-order frame

That makes it possible to connect:

- AI governance
- human accountability
- decision structure
- theology or first principles
- machine-readable artifact design

without collapsing them into one flat layer.
