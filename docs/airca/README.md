## Additional development

This node also includes **UAIRCA**, an extension developed by **Lowell T. Wong** that adds an explicit Ultimate Frame layer above the operational AIRCA architecture.

UAIRCA is designed to account for theological, philosophical, constitutional, mission-based, or other higher-order commitments that shape all lower decision architecture.

This node also includes **LAIRCA**, where **Logos** serves as an explicitly Christian example of the Ultimate Frame. In this expression, the higher-order layer is grounded in Christ as Logos, the source of truth, order, meaning, and moral coherence, with all lower decision architecture operating downstream from that theological frame.

Together, UAIRCA and LAIRCA clarify that AIRCA does not always begin at a neutral operational layer. It may instead operate downstream from an ultimate frame that is explicit, governing, and formative for all lower criteria, constraints, and commitments.
