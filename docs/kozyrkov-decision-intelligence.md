# Cassie Kozyrkov and Decision Intelligence

Cassie Kozyrkov is relevant to AIRCA because her public pedagogy consistently emphasizes turning information into better actions.

## What AIRCA should take from that lineage

AIRCA should center:

- the decision, not just the data
- human responsibility for framing
- careful use of automation
- explicit tradeoffs
- documentation of reasoning
- better judgment, not just better prediction

## AIRCA synthesis

AIRCA extends that direction by adding:

- machine-readable decision artifacts
- recursive decision structure
- human review and escalation logic
- white-paper generation from real decisions
- learning loops that preserve decision memory

## Practical implication

AIRCA should feel like an implementation layer for decision intelligence in GitHub-native environments.
