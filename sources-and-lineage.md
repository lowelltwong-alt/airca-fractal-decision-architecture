# Sources and Lineage

## Purpose

This file distinguishes among:
- intellectual influences
- collaborative development
- original contributions in this repository

## Intellectual influences

The broader thinking in this repository is informed in part by:

- **Cassie Kozyrkov** on decision intelligence and decision quality
- **Herbert Simon** on bounded rationality and limits on human decision capacity
- **Richard Susskind** on AI and institutional change

These thinkers influenced the surrounding problem space, but they are not being cited here as originators of AIRCA or of the fractal extensions developed in this repository.

## Collaborative development of AIRCA

The AIRCA framework emerged through a collaborative process between Alex A. and Lowell Wong.

It took shape through:
- an artifact created by Lowell Wong
- Alex A.’s interpretation and interrogation of that artifact through AI
- the interaction between the document’s structure, research content, and linked JSON, which helped connect and surface ideas across the artifact

This repository does not present AIRCA as having emerged in isolation.

## Original contributions in this repository

This repository particularly develops Lowell Wong’s extension of the AIRCA framework into:

- fractal taxonomy
- fractal ontology
- fractal decision architecture
- recursive decision structures that scale across levels
- machine-readable artifact design for governance and interpretation
